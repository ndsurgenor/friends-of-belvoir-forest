Getting to Belvoir Park Forest

by Car

From Belfast, turn left from A55 to Old Milltown Road then left and right to Belvoir Drive. From A55 other direction, turn right on to Belvoir Drive. Turn at the entrance signposted on Belvoir Drive (free parking available).

by Bus

Translink Metro services 6c & 6d stop 5 mins away from the forest entrance every 20mins at peak times.
