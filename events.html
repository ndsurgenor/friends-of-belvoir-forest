What's On and When?

Friends of Belvoir Park run a variety of weekend programmes throughout the year so there is always something to suit anyone who is interested caring for our environment. Upcoming events are shown in this section while a general overview of the year is proivided below.

CLICK HERE to view a detailed map of the forest

Clean-Up Day

Sat 3rd Sep 10.30

Meet in the car park before we head to Corbie Wood to remove litter and other debris. Bags and gloves provided. Sturdy footwear advised.

Lagan Trail 3km

Sat 10th Sep 10.30

Meet in the car park before we walk together through Corbie Wood the Meadow and alongside the Lagan. Suitable for young and old walkers alike.

Green Action

1st, 3rd & 5th Sat every month

meet in car park @ 10.30

Green Action events are at the very heart of what we do, wheter it's making nesting boxes for local wildlife or simply collecting litter. These are family friendly events as we look not only to inspire this generation but the next.

Woodland Walks

2nd & 4th Sat every month

meet in car park @ 10.30

While we want to look after our green spaces we also want to take time to enjoy them. Come and walk with us as we take in the beauty of our surroundings togther.

Community Cuppa

meet in The Village @ 11.00

Seminar Series

as arranged per term

meet in RSPB building

The newly refurbed RSPB building next to the car park provides the perfect space for our seminar series, featuring local experts and conservation enthusiasts covering a variety of ecological subjects.

Getting to Belvoir Park Forest

by Car

From Belfast, turn left from A55 to Old Milltown Road then left and right to Belvoir Drive. From A55 other direction, turn right straight on to Belvoir Drive. Turn at the entrance signposted on Belvoir Drive (free parking is available).

by Bus

Translink Metro services 6c & 6d stop 5 mins away from the forest entrance every 20mins at peak times.
