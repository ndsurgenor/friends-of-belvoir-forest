Why Join Us?

be Green

Take an active role in caring for local woodland through tree-planting, community cleanups, birdbox making, and much more designed with all ages in mind.

be Active

Engage with the outdoors, not only through conservation, but by walking or running the many miles of forest trails that Belvoir Park Forest has to offer.

be Social

Passions are better when we share them with others. Be a part of our community as you enjoy the woodland - or even just a cup of your favourite hot drink - together.

be Inspired

Learn more about caring for our earth as you hear from conservational experts and have the opportunity to ask them questions.

How to find Belvoir Park

by Car

From A55 Belfast direction, turn left on to Old Milltown Road then left and right on to Belvoir Drive. From A55 other directions, turn right straight on to Belvoir Drive. Turn at the entrance signposted on Belvoir Drive (free parking is available).

by Bus

Translink Metro services 6c & 6d stop 5 mins away from the forest entrance every 20mins at peak times.
