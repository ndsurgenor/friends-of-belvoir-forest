Why Join Us?

be Green

Take an active role in caring for local woodland through tree-planting, community cleanups, birdbox making and more, designed with all ages in mind.

be Active

Engage with the outdoors, not only through conservation, but by walking or running the many miles of forest trails that Belvoir Park Forest has to offer.

be Social

Passions are better when we share them with others. Be part of our community, whether it's enjoying the woodland or a favourite cuppa, together.

be Inspired

Learn more about caring for our earth as you hear from conservational experts and have the opportunity to ask your own questions.

Getting to Belvoir Park Forest

by Car

From Belfast, turn left from A55 to Old Milltown Road then left and right to Belvoir Drive. From A55 other direction, turn right on to Belvoir Drive. Turn at the entrance signposted on Belvoir Drive (free parking available).

by Bus

Translink Metro services 6c & 6d stop 5 mins away from the forest entrance every 20mins at peak times.
