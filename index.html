Why Join Us?

be Green

You can take an active role in looking after the local woodland through one of our GREEN ACTION days. Activities include tree-planting, community cleanups, birdbox making, and many more. All activities have been designed with both adults and children in mind.

be Active

Engaging with the outdoors is not only good for the health of our woodland but for your health too. Come and join one of our WOODLAND WALKS so you can enjoy the beauty of nature as your reward for helping to look after it.

be Social

Joining us means that you'll be joining our community. We have a broad range of members, young and old, from many different walks of life who take part in the activities and our COMMUNITY CUPPA days give you more time to take a break and know them better.

be Inspired

Being a part of our community gives you unique access to talks delivered by conservation experts as part of our INSPIRE SERIES. Come and hear how your particpation is having a real-world impact on the environment around us and take the opportunity to expand your knowledge.
